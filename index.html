My Projects

Ring Runner

An endless runner game project in which we made our own spaceship using 3ds Max. Although it seems the space ship is flying around the ring, in truth, in Unity it is the world which revolves around the player, creating the illusion of motion.

Gun.Smoke Remake

A group project that had us, a group of 4 people, recreate an old game. In this project I was the lead programmer and I made the enemy A.I, code for destructible objects, dynamite that explodes, and the code for both enemies and player to throw said dynamite towards their target in an arc.

Project Commando

A work in progress of making a stealth tactics game like the old Commando's game. In this project the main hurdles were making a vision cone for the enemy that shows his line of sight. In said project I also made an enemy patrol system that goes over the enemies actions and does them in order.

Bloody Mess

A game made for the Brackeys Game Jam in under a week with a group of 4 people. In this game you play a janitor trying to find and save his apprentice during a zombie apocalypse. I was a programmer, and I made the enemy movment, A.I, and implemented the animations from the sprites given to me by the artist.
